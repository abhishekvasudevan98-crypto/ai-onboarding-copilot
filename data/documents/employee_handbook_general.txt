1. Purpose

This handbook provides employees with essential information about workplace policies, operational systems, and everyday procedures at Honey’s Consulting Group (“HCG”).

The objective of this handbook is to help employees quickly understand:

• Company systems and tools
• Payroll and compensation timing
• Leave and attendance processes
• Expense submission procedures
• Where to find company policies
• Who to contact for operational support

This handbook supplements official company policies and governance documents.

2. Payroll & Salary Payments

Employees are paid once per month.

Payroll is processed on the last working day of each month.

Payments are processed through the Workday HRIS payroll system and deposited directly into the employee’s registered bank account.

Employees may view salary statements and payroll information within the Workday system.

3. Leave & Attendance Management

Employee attendance and leave management are handled through the Workday HRIS system.

Employees must submit leave requests through Workday.

Leave requests must be approved by the employee’s direct manager before the leave is confirmed.

Types of leave available may include:

• Annual leave
• Sick leave
• Personal leave
• Approved unpaid leave

Employees should submit leave requests as early as possible to ensure operational coverage.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
3.1 How to Request Time Off
--------------------------------------------------------------------

Employees can request time off by submitting a leave request through the Workday HRIS system.

Steps to request time off:

1. Log into Workday.
2. Navigate to the Leave Management section.
3. Select "Request Leave".
4. Choose the type of leave and the requested dates.
5. Submit the request for manager approval.

A leave request becomes approved only after the employee’s direct manager reviews and approves the request.

Employees are encouraged to submit time-off requests in advance whenever possible to ensure adequate team coverage.

4. Expense Submission & Reimbursement

Employees must submit business expenses using the Expensify expense management system.

Expensify is used for:

• Expense submission
• Reimbursement tracking
• Manager approval workflows

Employees must attach receipts and provide a business justification for all expenses.

Expenses must generally be submitted within 45 days of the expense date.

5. Company Systems & Tools

Honey’s Consulting Group uses several official enterprise systems.

Employees should only use approved corporate platforms for business activities.

Primary systems include:

Workday
Used for payroll, leave management, and employee records.

Expensify
Used for submitting and tracking employee expenses.

Salesforce
Used for customer relationship management and client opportunity tracking.

Jira
Used for project management, task tracking, and risk documentation.

Confluence
Used as the official knowledge repository for company documentation and policies.

6. Company Policies & Documentation

All official company policies are stored in Confluence.

Employees may access policies including:

• Code of Conduct
• Finance policies
• Compliance policies
• IT security policies
• Operational procedures

Confluence serves as the authoritative source for internal company documentation.

7. IT Support & Security

Employees must follow company IT security policies.

Key security requirements include:

• Use of multi-factor authentication (MFA)
• Secure password management
• Use of corporate VPN for remote access

Employees must report any suspected security incidents to the IT Security team immediately.

8. Reporting Concerns or Issues

Employees may report concerns related to workplace conduct, compliance issues, or operational problems through:

• Their direct manager
• The Human Resources department
• The Compliance Office

Honey’s Consulting Group maintains a non-retaliation policy for employees who report concerns in good faith.

9. Where to Find Help

Employees seeking assistance may contact:

Human Resources – employment questions, leave, payroll
Finance – expense and reimbursement questions
IT Support – system access and technical issues
Compliance – ethics or regulatory concerns
